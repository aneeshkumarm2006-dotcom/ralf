The Ralf

Guest House · Old Montréal

House of

Ralf

Located in the heart of Montreal's Old Port, just moments from City Hall, we offer an elevated hospitality experience where contemporary luxury meets the city's historic charm. Every stay is designed to provide remarkable experiences and memorable moments inspired by Montreal's rich design heritage, vibrant culture, and timeless character.

Meet Ralf

Rooms

Ten rooms. One penthouse.

We opt for fewer keys, so every room is spacious and comfortable — each with a kitchenette and living space, proper bedding, and considered light. Home comforts, with a luxe feel.

The Bar & Table

Spaces made for lingering

Inviting communal spaces encourage guests to linger, relax, and unwind together — a bar that draws the right crowd without trying too hard, and a long table that fills up by morning.

The Bar
