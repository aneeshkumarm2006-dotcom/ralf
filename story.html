The House

Meet Ralf

164 Notre-Dame Est, Old Montréal

No one is quite sure when Ralf first arrived at 164 Notre-Dame Est. Some say he's always been here.

We believe a guesthouse should feel like an arrival, and a return.

We keep fewer keys, so every guest is known, even if only for a few days.

We celebrate Old Montréal: its makers, its mornings, and its quiet corners.

Here, history isn't behind glass. It lives in the walls, the keys, and the way you're welcomed at the door.
