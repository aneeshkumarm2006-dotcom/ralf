About The Ralf

The doorman who never left

No one is entirely sure when Ralf first arrived at 164 Notre-Dame Est. Some say he's always been here, the kind of doorman who doesn't just open doors, but remembers who last passed through them.

Our Mission

A home away from home

The Ralf treats every guest to a home away from home experience. By opting for fewer keys, we make each room spacious, luxurious, and comfortable. Inviting communal spaces encourage guests to linger, relax, and unwind together.

Whether it's your first stay or your fifth, our mission is simple: make you feel known and welcomed, every time.

Our Vision

Old Montréal's definitive guesthouse

To become Old Montréal's definitive guesthouse, the antidote to impersonal, mass-market hotels and the gap left by Airbnb. We envision The Ralf as the benchmark for what intimate, design-led hospitality should feel like.

A place where history isn't kept behind glass, but lives in the walls, the keys, and the way you're welcomed at the door.

Our Purpose

Comfort, with polish

We exist for the traveller who chooses hotels the way others choose neighbourhoods, intentionally. Affluent, well-travelled, design-conscious guests who want to feel restored, not removed; inspired, not overstimulated.

The perfect balance between Airbnb's comfort and a hotel's polish: home comforts with a luxe feel, rooted authentically in Montréal through local artisans and suppliers.

Our Goal

What we're building

  • 01Maintain an intimate, guesthouse feel with only 10 rooms, including one 2,000 sq. ft. penthouse.
  • 02Deliver design-led spaces that are inspiring, carefully curated, and quietly surprising.
  • 03Honour our centrally located heritage building, putting guests steps from the best of Old Montréal.
  • 04Build a reputation for warm, personal, discreet service that keeps guests coming back.
