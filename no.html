A landmark hotel in downtown New York

Housed in an iconic Lower East Side landmark, Nine Orchard has meticulously restored and reimagined the century-old Jarmulowsky Bank into an elegant hotel and culinary destination.

Alive with original, Neo-Renaissance details and novel craftsmanship, our rooms offer guests a home-away-from-home in the heart of downtown New York. We look forward to welcoming you.

Originally built in 1912, the former Jarmulowsky Bank has been lovingly restored for travelers and locals alike.

Intimate spaces

Each of our 113 residential-inspired guest rooms offers a quiet retreat from the New York City streets right outside our doors. Discover considered details, from original moldings and distinctive artisanship to large windows that fill the space with natural light.

Explore Rooms & Suites

A culinary destination

The first floor features two distinct culinary experiences: Corner Bar, our comfortable all-day restaurant inspired by classic taverns; and Swan Room, a grand living room-by-day and lavish cocktail lounge by night.

Acclaimed for creative renditions of classic dishes, Corner Bar, Swan Room and the room service menu bring a signature simplicity and elegance to Nine Orchard.

  • Corner Bar

    A welcoming neighborhood stop, Corner Bar pairs thoughtfully-prepared bistro dishes with a vast selection of domestic wines and spirits. Come for a relaxing breakfast and return for a lively dinner or nightcap at our 40-seat bar.

    Learn more about Corner Bar

  • Swan Room

    Found in the former bank teller room — with its vaulted ceiling, luxurious booths, and pink Tennessee marble walls — the Swan Room offers an opulent, old-world setting to enjoy a quintessential New York evening of martinis and small plates.

    Learn more about Swan Room

Extraordinary celebrations

Suited to a variety of occasions, Nine Orchard’s three event spaces are designed for gatherings both small and large. We collaborate with each host to create a unique and unforgettable celebration.
